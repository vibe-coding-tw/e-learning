# Funding Roadmap (F/Angel -> Seed/Pre-A -> A)

Last updated: 2026-05-23

## 1) Stage Plan

### Stage 0: F/Angel (0-12 months)
Objective:
- Validate paid demand and retention loop.
- Prove tutor-assignment + autograde + referral-sharing workflow can run at scale.

Core KPIs:
- Monthly paid learners
- Monthly revenue and MoM growth
- 30/90-day retention
- Course completion rate
- Tutor-linked order ratio

Capital use:
- Product stabilization and growth experiments
- Core content production
- First repeatable acquisition channel

Fundraise signal:
- 3+ consecutive months of measurable growth
- At least one stable acquisition loop

### Stage 1: Seed / Pre-A (12-24 months)
Objective:
- Prove repeatable growth with healthy unit economics.

Core KPIs:
- CAC / LTV
- Payback period
- Gross margin
- Refund rate
- Active tutor productivity (students per tutor, outcomes)

Capital use:
- Build growth and ops team
- Expand B2B/B2B2C partnerships
- Improve content delivery throughput

Fundraise signal:
- Predictable conversion funnel
- Unit economics close to break-even or better

### Stage 2: A Round (24-36 months)
Objective:
- Scale predictably across segments/regions.

Core KPIs:
- ARR/MRR predictability
- Net revenue retention
- Multi-channel growth reliability
- Operational efficiency by cohort

Capital use:
- Regional expansion
- Enterprise programs
- Leadership and governance upgrade

Fundraise signal:
- Repeatable growth with disciplined financial controls

## 2) Milestone Checklist

### F/Angel readiness checklist
- [ ] Product core loops stable in production
- [ ] KPI dashboard baseline completed
- [ ] 12-month operating budget complete
- [ ] Initial investor deck completed

### Seed/Pre-A readiness checklist
- [ ] CAC/LTV tracked by channel
- [ ] Cohort retention dashboard in place
- [ ] Tutor quality control protocol standardized
- [ ] Forecast model tied to real pipeline

### A round readiness checklist
- [ ] Auditable financial process ready
- [ ] Organization plan for next 24 months
- [ ] Expansion strategy validated with pilot results

## 3) Metrics Governance

Single source of truth:
- Operational data: Firestore + dashboard exports
- Financial data: monthly closed statements

Cadence:
- Weekly: growth and product KPI review
- Monthly: board/investor metrics packet
- Quarterly: stage readiness assessment
