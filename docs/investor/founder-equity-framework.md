# Founder Equity Framework

Last updated: 2026-05-23

## 1) Principles
- Equity follows long-term contribution and accountability.
- Roles and decision rights are explicit before final allocation.
- Vesting is mandatory for all founders.

## 2) Recommended Early Cap Structure
- Founder group: 55%-75%
- ESOP pool: 10%-15%
- Advisor pool: 1%-3%
- Investor allocation: staged by rounds

## 3) Vesting Standard
- 4-year vesting
- 1-year cliff
- Monthly vest after cliff
- Unvested shares repurchased at departure

## 4) Role Architecture
- Founder A: CEO / product-market / fundraising
- Founder B: CTO / system architecture / data
- Founder C: curriculum and learning outcomes
- Optional Founder D: growth/channel partnerships

Each founder should have:
- Explicit scope ownership
- Quarterly measurable objectives
- Clear escalation and decision boundaries

## 5) Founder Agreement Clauses (must-have)
- IP assignment to company
- Confidentiality and non-compete (where legally applicable)
- Time commitment expectation (full-time/part-time timeline)
- Voting rights and reserved matters
- Bad leaver / good leaver treatment
- Dispute resolution mechanism

## 6) Governance Rhythm
- Weekly execution sync
- Monthly strategy and KPI review
- Quarterly founder role review

## 7) Rebalancing Policy
- Avoid frequent cap table rewrites.
- Use option grants for incremental contribution adjustments.
- Re-open founder equity only on major role redefinition.
