## 前言 / Preamble

鑑於甲方經營 Vibe Coding 線上學習暨軟硬體整合平台，乙方為具備在地倉儲、出貨與物流履行能力之專業經銷商；
雙方同意依據甲方平台之「平台統一收款、經銷商在地履約」模式進行合作，並就實體商品之分派、配送、退換貨及缺貨責任歸屬等事項，特立本協議以共同遵守。

Whereas, Party A operates the Vibe Coding online learning and hardware-integrated platform, and Party B is a professional distributor with local warehousing, shipping, and logistics fulfillment capabilities;
The Parties agree to cooperate under Party A’s model of "Centralized Platform Billing & Localized Distributor Fulfillment," and have entered into this Agreement to govern the dispatch, delivery, refund, return, and stockout liabilities of physical products.

## 第一條：合作模式 / Article 1: Cooperation Model

### 1.1 平台統一收款 / Centralized Platform Billing
所有終端客戶（以下簡稱「客戶」）於 Vibe Coding 平台購買之課程、硬體或合購方案，其交易款項一律由甲方平台之金流閘道（Payment Gateway）統一收取，並由甲方開立發票或收據予客戶。乙方不得自行向平台客戶收取實體硬體之銷售款項。
All transaction payments for courses, hardware, or bundled packages purchased by end customers (hereinafter referred to as "Customers") on the Vibe Coding platform shall be centrally collected by Party A through its payment gateways. Party A shall issue invoices or receipts to Customers. Party B shall not directly collect hardware sales payments from Customers.

### 1.2 經銷商在地履約 / Localized Distributor Fulfillment
凡訂單中包含實體商品（如硬體套件、配件等），甲方系統將依據客戶所屬地區或綁定關係，將履約任務派發予乙方。乙方應負責其指定區域內之商品保管、包裝、出貨及配送至客戶指定地點。
For any orders containing physical products (e.g., hardware kits, accessories), Party A’s system will dispatch fulfillment tasks to Party B based on the Customer's region or tutor binding. Party B shall be responsible for product storage, packaging, shipping, and delivery to the designated location of Customers within its assigned territory.

## 第二條：訂單分派與出貨時效 (SLA) / Article 2: Order Dispatch & Fulfillment SLA

### 2.1 訂單狀態與工作流 / Order States and Workflow
雙方同意遵循甲方系統定義之履約狀態進行作業：
The Parties agree to operate in accordance with the fulfillment states defined by Party A’s system:
1. `ASSIGNED`（已指派經銷商）：甲方平台完成收款，並指派訂單予乙方。 / Party A completes collection and assigns the order to Party B.
2. `ACCEPTED`（經銷商已接單）：乙方確認庫存並接受履約指派。 / Party B verifies stock and accepts the assignment.
3. `PACKING`（備貨中）：乙方進行撿貨、包裝與物流標籤列印。 / Party B performs picking, packing, and shipping label printing.
4. `SHIPPED`（已出貨）：乙方將商品交付物流商並回寫物流單號。 / Party B hands over products to the carrier and uploads the tracking number.
5. `DELIVERED`（已送達）：物流商配送完成或客戶確認收妥。 / The carrier completes delivery or the Customer confirms receipt.

### 2.2 出貨時效限制 / Shipping SLA
* **接單時效**：乙方應於收到訂單指派（`ASSIGNED`）後 **24 小時內** 確認接受訂單（變更為 `ACCEPTED`）。
  **Order Acceptance SLA**: Party B shall accept the assigned order (change status to `ACCEPTED`) within **24 hours** after receiving the assignment (`ASSIGNED`).
* **出貨時效**：乙方應於接單（`ACCEPTED`）後 **2 個工作天內** 完成出貨（變更為 `SHIPPED`）並上傳有效之物流追蹤單號（Tracking Number）。
  **Fulfillment SLA**: Party B shall complete shipment (change status to `SHIPPED`) and upload a valid tracking number within **2 business days** after accepting the order (`ACCEPTED`).
* 因不可抗力之事由（如天災、罷工等）導致延遲者，乙方應立即於系統備註欄位（`fulfillmentNotes`）向甲方回報並說明。
  In the event of delays caused by force majeure (e.g., natural disasters, strikes), Party B shall immediately report and explain to Party A via the system notes field (`fulfillmentNotes`).

## 第三條：退款與退貨機制（三方責任） / Article 3: Refund and Return Policy (Three-Party Liability)

因甲方統一收款並提供線上課程授權，實體商品退換貨衍生之款項退還、逆向物流與折舊責任，依下列約定辦理：
Since Party A centrally collects payments and grants course access, refunds, reverse logistics, and depreciation arising from physical product returns shall be handled as follows:

### 3.1 客戶無條件退貨權利（鑑賞期） / Customer’s Statutory Return Right (Cooling-off Period)
* 客戶依據在地法律（如台灣消費者保護法之七天猶豫期）向甲方申請退票/退貨時，實體商品應退還至乙方指定之退貨地址。
  When a Customer applies to Party A for a refund/return under local laws (such as Taiwan's 7-day cooling-off period), the physical product must be returned to Party B's designated return address.
* 逆向物流費用負擔：
  **Reverse Logistics Shipping Cost**:
  - 若屬非商品瑕疵之無條件退貨，退貨運費由客戶自行負擔，或依甲方平台政策約定；
    If the return is an unconditional return without product defects, the return shipping cost shall be borne by the Customer, or as governed by Party A's platform policies;
  - 若退貨係因商品瑕疵、寄錯商品等乙方責任所致，退貨運費由乙方全額負擔。
    If the return is caused by Party B's fault (e.g., defective goods, incorrect shipment), the return shipping cost shall be borne entirely by Party B.

### 3.2 退款處理與折舊判定 / Refund Processing and Depreciation Assessment
* 乙方於收到退回之實體商品後，應於 **3 個工作天內** 進行開箱檢驗，並將檢驗結果登錄於系統。
  Upon receiving the returned physical product, Party B shall inspect it within **3 business days** and record the inspection results in the system.
* 商品狀況判定標準：
  **Product Condition Standard**:
  - **全新未拆封 / Merchantable Condition**：乙方確認商品無損且包裝完整，可重新上架銷售。甲方將扣除原出貨處理費後，將剩餘銷售款項退還客戶，並取消該筆乙方之履約結算。
    If Party B confirms the product is undamaged and in its original sealed packaging, it is fit for resale. Party A shall refund the remaining payment to the Customer, deduct the original handling fee (if applicable), and cancel Party B's fulfillment settlement for the order.
  - **已拆封/有使用痕跡/損壞 / Opened, Used, or Damaged**：若商品包裝已毀損或有明顯使用痕跡，導致無法以新品再次銷售：
    If the product packaging is damaged or shows clear signs of use, rendering it unsellable as new:
    1. 乙方應提供照片及檢驗報告，並由雙方依比例向客戶主張折舊折讓扣款（例如售價之 10% ~ 30%）。
       Party B shall provide photos and inspection reports, and the Parties shall mutually assess depreciation charges (e.g., 10% to 30% of the retail price) to be deducted from the Customer's refund.
    2. 折舊扣除之金額應於對帳時補償予乙方。
       The deducted depreciation amount shall be compensated to Party B during the monthly settlement.

## 第四條：缺貨與履約延遲責任 / Article 4: Stockout and Delay Liability

乙方作為在地庫存與履約主體，應維持安全庫存，並對因乙方原因導致之履約失敗承擔責任。
As the local inventory and fulfillment entity, Party B shall maintain safety stock levels and bear liability for any fulfillment failures caused by Party B.

### 4.1 缺貨通報 SLA / Stockout Notification SLA
* 若乙方發現因系統庫存不準確或臨時缺貨而無法履行指派之訂單，乙方必須於訂單指派後 **24 小時內** 通知甲方，以便甲方客服進行退款或轉單處理。
  If Party B discovers it cannot fulfill an assigned order due to inventory discrepancy or temporary stockout, Party B must notify Party A within **24 hours** of order assignment, so that Party A can process refunds or re-route the order.
* 未能於 SLA 內通報而造成客戶糾紛或甲方損失者，乙方應賠償甲方因此支出之處理手續費或補償金。
  If Party B fails to notify Party A within the SLA, resulting in Customer disputes or losses to Party A, Party B shall compensate Party A for any handling fees or customer compensation paid.

### 4.2 延遲與缺貨之懲罰性賠償 / Penalties for Delay and Stockout
* **缺貨轉單/取消**：因乙方缺貨導致訂單需取消退款者，每筆訂單乙方應向甲方支付相當於該商品售價 **10%** 之違約行政處理費。
  **Order Cancellation due to Stockout**: If an order is cancelled or refunded due to Party B's stockout, Party B shall pay Party A a breach penalty equivalent to **10%** of the product's retail price as administrative handling fees per order.
* **嚴重延遲**：乙方逾指派後 **5 個工作天** 仍未出貨（且無不可抗力原因），甲方有權單方面強制取消該訂單，並比照缺貨取消條款向乙方收取違約費用。
  **Severe Delay**: If Party B fails to ship an order within **5 business days** after assignment (without force majeure), Party A has the right to unilaterally cancel the order and charge Party B the breach penalty as described in the stockout clause.

## 第五條：財務結算與費用對帳 / Article 5: Settlement & Fees

### 5.1 結算基礎 / Settlement Basis
雙方結算以甲方系統中，訂單狀態為 `SHIPPED`（已出貨）或 `DELIVERED`（已送達），且未於對帳週期內發生退貨之訂單為基礎。
The billing settlement shall be based on orders in Party A's system that are marked as `SHIPPED` or `DELIVERED`, and have not been returned during the settlement period.

### 5.2 結算與對帳週期 / Settlement Period & Reconciliation
* 雙方約定以 **[每月 / 每週]** 為一個對帳週期。甲方應於週期結束後 **5 個工作天內** 提供乙方對帳報表。
  The Parties agree to use a **[monthly / weekly]** reconciliation cycle. Party A shall provide the reconciliation report to Party B within **5 business days** after the end of each cycle.
* 乙方確認報表無誤後，應開立符合在地稅法之發票，甲方於收到發票後 **[30] 天內** 將結算款項匯入乙方指定之銀行帳戶。
  Upon confirming the correctness of the report, Party B shall issue tax-compliant invoices. Party A shall transfer the settled amount to Party B's designated bank account within **[30] days** of receiving the invoice.

### 5.3 費用結構 / Fee Structure
每筆履約完成之訂單，結算金額計算如下：
For each successfully fulfilled order, the settled amount shall be calculated as follows:

$$\text{結算款項} = \text{經銷商底價 (Dealer Cost)} + \text{代配運費 (Shipping Cost)} + \text{出貨處理費 (Handling Fee)} + \text{服務分潤 (Service Share)} - \text{退款折抵 / 扣款 (Refund Deductions/Penalties)}$$
$$\text{Settled Amount} = \text{Dealer Cost} + \text{Shipping Cost} + \text{Handling Fee} + \text{Service Share} - \text{Refund Deductions/Penalties}$$

* **經銷商底價 (Dealer Cost)**：指乙方採購或提供該硬體之約定基本價格。 / The agreed base price for Party B purchasing or providing the hardware.
* **代配運費 (Shipping Cost)**：由乙方實際支付物流商之配送運費（依據對帳憑證）。 / The actual shipping costs paid by Party B to carriers (subject to reconciliation vouchers).
* **出貨處理費 (Handling Fee)**：每筆實體訂單之包裝、出庫基本處理費用，約定為每筆新台幣 / 美金 ________ 元。 / The picking, packing, and warehousing fee for each physical order, agreed at ________ per order.
* **服務分潤 (Service Share)**：若該訂單涉及乙方綁定導師之服務費分潤，應依甲方系統記錄之 `revenue_share_policies` 費率另行計入。 / If the order involves service revenue sharing for tutors bound to Party B, it shall be calculated in accordance with the `revenue_share_policies` rates recorded in Party A's system.

## 第六條：客戶隱私與資料保護 / Article 6: Customer Privacy and Data Protection

### 6.1 資訊安全承諾 / Information Security Commitment
為履行本協議之物流派單與出貨，甲方將向乙方揭露客戶之個人資料（包括姓名、電話、配送地址、購買品項等，以下簡稱「個資」）。
To facilitate order dispatch and delivery, Party A will disclose Customer personal data (including name, telephone, delivery address, and purchased items; hereinafter referred to as "Personal Data") to Party B.

### 6.2 個資使用限制 / Use Restriction
* 乙方承諾僅得將甲方提供之個資用於**本協議約定之物流出貨與在地售後服務**目的。不得將個資用於行銷、推銷、或轉售予任何第三人。
  Party B covenants that it shall use the Personal Data provided by Party A solely for the purpose of **fulfillment shipping and local after-sales services** under this Agreement. Party B shall not use the Personal Data for marketing, promotion, or transfer it to any third party.
* 乙方應於訂單完成履約或終止合作後，依甲方要求或本國個資法規定，安全地銷毀或返還相關客戶個資，不得留存備份。
  Upon completion of fulfillment or termination of cooperation, Party B shall, at Party A's request or in accordance with local data protection laws, securely destroy or return Customer Personal Data and shall not retain any copies.

## 第七條：爭議解決與管轄法律 / Article 7: Dispute Resolution and Governing Law

### 7.1 管轄法律 / Governing Law
本協議之解釋、效力及履行，均以 **[中華民國 / 經銷商所在地國家]** 法律為準據法。
The interpretation, validity, and performance of this Agreement shall be governed by the laws of **[Republic of China (Taiwan) / Country of Distributor's Location]**.

### 7.2 訴訟管轄 / Jurisdictional Court
因本協議所生之任何爭議，雙方應先本於誠信原則友好協商。如協商不成而提起訴訟，雙方同意以 **[台灣台北地方法院 / 雙方約定法院]** 為第一審管轄法院。
Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall first be resolved through good-faith negotiation. If negotiation fails and litigation is commenced, the Parties agree to submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the **[Taiwan Taipei District Court / Mutually Agreed Court]** as the court of first instance.
